Have you ever played Pokemon and wondered which Pokemon to use? Well, this tier list explains which Pokemon to use and which ones to avoid!

Starter Pokemon

Your starter Pokemon is the very first Pokemon you receive. They are some of the most important Pokemon, so it is important to choose the right one. We will ignore Mega Pokemon, since some Pokemon do not have Mega Evolutions. Mega Pokemon

In addition to starter Pokemon, Mega Pokemon are also important. Ever since Generation 6, many Pokemon have gotten Mega Evolutions, which are more powerful than the Pokemon before the Mega Evolution. Because they are so powerful, you are limited to one per team, so choose wisely. Directions

  • The ratings are based off the official competitive tiers.
  • Please make sure to spell everything correctly.
  • If you enter a Mega Pokemon in a non-Mega slot, it will be treated as the original non-Mega Pokemon instead.
  • Alternatively, you can include non-Mega Pokemon in the Mega Pokemon slot.
  • Pokemon with separate forms should be entered as name-form, for example, "Necrozma-Ultra", "Deoxys-Attack", or "Sivally-Fairy". (You do not have to do this with Mega Pokemon and Alolan Pokemon, although you can).
  • Specify the Mega Form for Mega Mewtwo and Mega Charizard, for example, "Mewtwo-Mega-Y" or "Mega Charizard X"
  • Arceus' other forms are not included in here.
