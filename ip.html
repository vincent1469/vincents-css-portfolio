Starter Pokemon

Your starter Pokemon is the very first Pokemon you receive. They are some of the most important Pokemon, so it is important to choose the right one. We will ignore Mega Pokemon, since some Pokemon do not have Mega Evolutions. Hover over the Pokemon for a short description of why it's in that tier, and click on that Pokemon to take it to its Bulbapedia page.

Directions

  • The ratings are based off the official competitive tiers.
  • Please make sure to spell everything correctly.
  • If you enter a Mega Pokemon in a non-Mega slot, it will be treated as the original Pokemon.
  • Alternatively, you can include non-Mega Pokemon in the Mega Pokemon slot.
  • Pokemon with separate forms should be entered as name-form, for example, Rayquaza-Mega, Deoxys-Attack, or Sandslash-Alola.
  • Mega Mewtwo and Mega Charizard are written as Charizard-Mega-X, Charizard-Mega-Y, Mewtwo-Mega-X, Mewtwo-Mega-Y.
